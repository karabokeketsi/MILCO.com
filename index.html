Why Choose Us
More Than a Store

MILCO operates as a four-in-one platform: physical store, online marketplace, wholesaler, and marketing hub for local businesses.

Physical Store
Visit us at Sefika Complex, Maseru, where you can browse and purchase authentic Lesotho products in person.
Online Platform
Shop from anywhere across Lesotho and beyond. Our e-commerce platform brings Lesotho's best to your doorstep.
Wholesale Hub
Businesses can source quality Lesotho-made products in bulk. We support the full local supply chain.
Marketing Hub
MILCO amplifies local brands, connecting entrepreneurs with customers through targeted marketing and exposure.
NUL Innovation Hub
Founded by the National University of Lesotho Innovation Hub, MILCO is rooted in academic excellence and community upliftment.
Community First
Every purchase directly supports local artisans, farmers, and small businesses — keeping money in Lesotho's economy.
